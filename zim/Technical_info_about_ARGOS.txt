====== Technical Info About ARGOS ======

**Project Overview**

ARGOS is a comprehensive IoT security and autonomous vehicle project divided into two main components:

1. **ARGOS Application** - IoT security monitoring and management platform
2. **ARGOS Autonomous Car** - Smart vehicle system with sensors and autonomous capabilities

---

**Project Structure**

* `/argos-app/` - Application backend and frontend
* `/argos-car/` - Autonomous vehicle hardware and software
